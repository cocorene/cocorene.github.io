Chanelle White is a personal stylist turned web designer and front-end developer with a background in public relations. Chanelle relies on these skills to create stunning brands and websites for the fashion/beauty/lifestyle industry. When not designing and coding on her MacBook Pro, Chanelle enjoys sunny days at the beach, DIY projects, and catching up with decor and fashion blogs. Chanelle lives and works in Southern New Jersey.
