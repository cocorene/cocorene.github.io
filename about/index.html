Skillful Developer and Communicator

Chanelle White is a public relations strategist turned front-end designer, developer. Drawing on her creativity and analytical thinking, Chanelle creates stunning brand identities and dynamic websites for fashion creatives. She loves using WordPress for client projects and custom coding template pages and plugins for added functionality. When not coding on her MacBook Pro, Chanelle enjoys sunny days at the beach, DIY projects, and catching up with decor and fashion blogs. Chanelle lives and works in Southern New Jersey.
