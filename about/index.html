From Stylist, to Web Designer

Chanelle White is a personal stylist turned web designer and front-end developer. Drawing on her creativity and analytical mind, Chanelle creates stunning brand identities and websites for the fashion/beauty/lifestyle industry. Chanelle holds a BA in Communications, started her career in public relations, and branched out to run several businesses prior to building websites. When not designing and coding on her MacBook Pro, Chanelle enjoys sunny days at the beach, DIY projects, and catching up with decor and fashion blogs. Chanelle lives and works in Southern New Jersey.
